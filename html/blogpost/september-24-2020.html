Bootcamp Week 1: Front End

The first week of in-person boot camp started this week and I had a blast. I am in Ian’s classroom and is a great resource for any questions I have. I also have 3 other great classmates Rachel, Duncan, and Chandler, as well as Becky on Zoom.
